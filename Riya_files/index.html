Movement

Expressive Dancing

Kathak is one of the most beautiful classical dance forms from Northern India. It gets its name from the word "Katha," which means "story," and "Kathakar," which means "storyteller." Through graceful spins, rhythmic footwork, and expressive facial gestures, Kathak dancers tell magical stories of gods, kings, and nature.
